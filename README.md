# Foxie the Desktop Pet!

Foxie wanders around your screen, sleeps, plays with toys, gets hungry, grows older, and reacts to how you interact with him. Keep him happy, fed, and rested while watching him grow through different life stages.



## Features

1. Walks around your taskbar by himself
2. Sleeps when low on energy
3. Occasionally gets the zoomies
4. Reacts to pets and lots of other interactions
5. Pick him up by his neck and drag him around (he gets annoying at times)


## Happiness System

Click on his body to open up the main menu.

You will see 3 bars: Happiness, Hunger, and Energy, with 3 buttons beside them respectively.

Click on the [+] button near the Happiness bar and pet him (move your mouse left and right) near his ears or the top of his back.

He loves pets and will become happier!!

But be careful! Don't poke (click) his nose or little feet. He does not like it one bit!



## Feeding System

Click on the [+] button near the Hunger bar and voila! One of 5 food items will randomly spawn on your mouse.

Each food item has different effects on Foxie!

Wiggle the food item near his mouth and he will eat it.

Keep him fed—a happy fox is a full fox.



## Sleeping System

Foxie occasionally sleeps to regain energy, but you can make him sleep until he regains all of his energy by clicking on the [+] button near the Energy bar!



## Playtime Feature

On the main menu, click the red ball icon and it will spawn 1 of 3 possible toys.

These toys have physics, and Foxie will happily chase after them when you throw them.



## Stats System

Track Foxie's growth by clicking on the More button in the main menu.

Currently tracked:

1. Age
2. Weight
3. Phase
4. Size
5. Foods eaten
6. Hours slept
7. Times played
8. Total pets
9. Distance walked
10. Total achievements

Foxie grows older every 5 minutes.



## Achievements

All achievements for now:

1. First Nap
2. First Meal
3. First Playtime
4. First Pinch
5. First Zoomies

You can view all achievements by clicking on the main menu and then the green checkmark button.


## Important

Foxie's three main needs are:

1. Happiness
2. Hunger
3. Energy

If these get too low, Foxie becomes slower, sleeps more often, and becomes less active overall.

Take good care of him!



He has a very rare chance of doing something very, very unusual!!!!
